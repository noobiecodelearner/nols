Notes on Learning Systems

What does machine learning look like when treated as a living system, not a model?

Most writing about machine learning focuses on what happens inside the algorithm. This blog is about everything else: how systems age, how humans shape what models learn, how design choices echo through production, and how the infrastructure we build changes what becomes possible. It's not about debugging models—it's about understanding the systems they live in.
